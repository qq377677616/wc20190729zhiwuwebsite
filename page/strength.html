中科院昆明植物研究所植物医生研发中心 Kunming institute of botany, Chinese academy of sciences
中国民族植物学创始人裴盛基教授担任品牌首席科学家，带领二十多名中科院昆植所中青年研究员、博士生，运用现代基因技术，解密高山植物更多独特的护肤功效 Professor pei shengji, founder of Chinese ethnobotany, serves as the brand's chief scientist and leads more than 20 young and middle-aged researchers and doctoral students of kunzhi institute of Chinese academy of sciences to use modern genetic technology to reveal more unique skin care effects of alpine plants
北京·亚太皮肤科学研究所 Beijing asia-pacific institute of dermatology
专研亚洲黄种人（中国、日本、韩国）皮肤特性、皮肤需求及应对护肤方案，为亚太消费者提供高科技的高山植物护肤方案。 Specializing in the skin characteristics, skin needs and skincare solutions of Asian yellow people (China, Japan and South Korea), providing high-tech alpine plant skincare solutions for asia-pacific consumers.
东京·汉方护肤科学研究中心 Tokyo · hanfang skin care science research center
应用中国传统汉方仙草精粹，专研日本消费者喜爱的中国护肤产品，为品牌进入日本市场储备原料及配方技术方案。 Applying the essence of Chinese traditional hanfang xiancao, the company specializes in studying Chinese skin care products favored by Japanese consumers, and reserves raw materials and formula technical solutions for the brand to enter the Japanese market.
