BRAND STORY
DR PLANT植物医生是中国高山植物护肤品类的开创者，与中国科学院昆明植物研究所签订独家合作， DR PLANT is the pioneer of alpine PLANT skin care products in China. It has signed an exclusive cooperation with kunming institute of botany, Chinese academy of sciences.
并联合成立“植物医生研发中心”，与科学家们一起探索高山植物的美肌奥秘 and the joint establishment of "plant doctor research and development center", together with scientists to explore the beauty of mountain plants mystery

DR PLANT植物医生截止目前已拥有会员超过750万人，门店数量3015家，分布于中国313个城市。 DR PLANT has more than 7.5 million members and 3,015 stores in 313 cities in China.
DR PLANT植物医生热衷于公益事业，2017年启动“生物多样性-高山植物保护行动”， DR PLANT is passionate about public welfare. In 2017, he launched the "biodiversity - alpine PLANT conservation initiative".
在中国云南省5590亩的土地上开展生态环境恢复和保护工作。 ecological environment restoration and protection work will be carried out on 5,590 mu of land in yunnan province, China.
